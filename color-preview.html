Design Principles

Less, but better

The color palette embodies Rams' principle of restraint. Every color serves a purpose, nothing is decorative.

Good design is honest

Colors communicate function clearly. Orange indicates action, greys provide hierarchy, white creates space.

Good design is long-lasting

This timeless palette avoids trends, ensuring the interface remains fresh and professional for years.
